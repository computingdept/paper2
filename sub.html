Subroutine Trace Table Challenges

These 10 challenges involve procedures and functions commonly used in high school programming. Each question shows pseudocode with explicit line numbers. In the trace tables, enter the subprogram call (prefixed with "procedure" or "function" and with parameters if needed) and any outputs produced.

Q1: Simple Procedure Call

Scenario: A school trip requires a simple check. The procedure agePass() prints a message if a student is old enough to ride.

Pseudocode:

01 Procedure agePass()
02     print("You are old enough to ride")
03 endprocedure
